Let's go

I made the decision to go on a quick trip to Caza Peregrine in Antipolo, Rizal, on a sunny Saturday morning. The enthusiasm of exploring a new location was evident, the sun was shining, and the air was clear. I was eager to visit this little retreat, which I had heard so much about because of its calm atmosphere and stunning views.

Eager to get the most out of the day, I got up early. I grabbed my camera, a water bottle, and a small backpack full of goodies after a short breakfast. After consulting my maps, I made the decision to ride a jeepney from my home to the busy bus station in order to find a ride to Antipolo.

Caza Peregrine Resort and Restaurant

offers a unique opportunity to experience the beauty of the Philippine mountains without the strenuous climb. Located in San Mateo, Rizal, this mountainside retreat provides stunning views of the surrounding landscape, including the iconic sea of clouds that often blankets the area and a 360 degree view of the Sierra Madre mountains. Here's a quick overview of what Caza Peregrine offers:

  • Scenic Viewpoints: Enjoy panoramic views of the mountains and valleys from the resort's tree-top view deck.
  • Affordable Rates: The entrance fee is a reasonable ₱50, with discounted rates for children.
  • Delicious Filipino Cuisine: Savor authentic Filipino dishes like Crispy Pata and Sizzling Pork Sisig at the on-site restaurant.
  • Overnight Stays: For those seeking a longer stay, the resort offers comfortable accommodations, including the Kubo Room and the Glass Room.

The resort offers a variety of vantage points to take in the scenery. Relax by the pool and soak up the sun while enjoying the view, or take a hike up one of the trails to get a panoramic perspective. In the evening, be sure to stargaze from one of the resort's many decks. With minimal light pollution, the night sky comes alive with a dazzling display of stars. Whether you're a nature lover, a foodie, or simply looking for a peaceful getaway, Caza Peregrine is a must-visit destination in Rizal.

Plan Your Visit: Getting to Casa Peregrine is convenient, located just a short drive from Metro Manila. You can find detailed directions here.
